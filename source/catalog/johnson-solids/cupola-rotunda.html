Cupola & rotunda

The next four Johnson solids are three cupolae and one rotunda. They represent sections of uniform polyhedra.

  • Triangular Cupola (J3)

  • Square Cupola (J4)

  • Pentagonal Cupola (J5)

  • Pentagonal Rotunda (J6)
